# Artificial Intelligence in Diplomacy

This repository contains my research paper exploring the impact of Artificial Intelligence on diplomacy, foreign policy, and international relations.

## Author

Hamza Hassan

Computer Science Student  
Jordan University of Science and Technology (JUST)

## Abstract

This paper examines how Artificial Intelligence is transforming diplomatic practice through policy analysis, machine translation, predictive analytics, crisis management, and public diplomacy. It also discusses governance, cybersecurity, ethical challenges, and implications for Jordan's foreign policy.
